Janos Salamon

 

Janos Salamon is an organisational psychologist with experiences in large corporate, consultancy and startup world. Social learning and skill development were always in his focus, which led him to do his research in behaviour change intervention design and evaluation.

E-mail: salamon.jon at gmail.com

Education

  • 2016- PhD, Experimental Psychology, Faculty of Education and Psychology, Eotvos Loránd University, Budapest, Hungary
  • 2010- 2012 MA, Work and Organisational Psychology, Budapest University of Technology and Economics, Budapest, Hungary
    (dissertation: Mapping of Knowledge Sharing Willingness in Organizations.)
  • 2007- 2010 BA, Psychology, University of Szeged, Szeged, Hungary

Scientific Interest

  • scientific study of corporate learning and training effectiveness
  • behaviour change interventions in organisational context
  • knowledge management, corporate experience management
